Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean my carpet? +
Regular vacuuming is recommended. For deep cleaning, use carpet shampoo or steam cleaning. Avoid harsh chemicals. Professional cleaning is recommended every 6-12 months.
Do you offer installation services? +
Currently, we do not offer installation services. However, our carpets are easy to lay and come with instructions for best results.
What sizes are available? +
We have carpets ranging from small (230x160 cm) to extra large (200x300 cm). Custom sizes can also be ordered upon request.
How long does delivery take? +
Accra deliveries: 1 hours. Other regions in Ghana: 1 business day. Express delivery available for urgent orders.
Are the carpets durable? +
Yes! All our carpets are made from high-quality materials designed to last for years with proper care. Many come with anti-slip backing and stain-resistant features.
